Photography is one of my most active hobbies/side jobs. I love being able to grab my camera to capture the world as I may see it. Subjects range for me - I'll take photos of nature, newsy events, people, animals, buildings, landscapes. Name something photo worthy, and I'll try to capture the story in the best way possible. I currently take photos as a freelance photographer, for the Stanford Daily, and for my own purposes.
